Privacy Policy for Data Widget

Last Updated: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Data Widget ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information in relation to your use of the application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User-Provided Data

The app collects and stores the information you explicitly provide:

  • JSON URLs: The web addresses you enter to fetch data.
  • Configuration Settings: Names, colors, transparency levels, and font sizes associated with your widgets.

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

1.2 Data Fetched from URLs

The app fetches data from the URLs you provide to display it on your home screen. We do not inspect, log, or store this data on our servers. Please note that the servers hosting your JSON files may collect standard web server logs (e.g., your IP address, time of access).

2. Use of Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Display the requested data on your home screen widgets.
  • Remember your visual preferences (colors, fonts, etc.).

3. Foreground Service and Battery Optimization

The app uses a Foreground Service to maintain per-second updates for live counters. By default, updates are paused when the screen is off to save battery. Note that frequent requests to your provided URLs may be logged by your data provider.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising frameworks.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information. All configuration data remains within the app's private storage on your device. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through our GitHub repository.
